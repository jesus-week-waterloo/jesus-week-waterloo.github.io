A Holy and Perfect God

In the beginning, God created the world. He is a perfect and holy God who cannot be in the presence of anything short of perfection.

The Fall

Humans are sinful in nature and deserve God’s wrath, eternal punishment. We are eternally separated from God.

The Messiah

God sent his one and only Son Jesus to die on the cross as the perfect sacrifice, to satisfy God’s wrath. And on the third day, Jesus rose and conquered death.

Saved by Grace Through Faith

Though we hated him, God gives to us unmerited favour by sending his own Son to die in our place. It is because of him, not of any earned merit of ourselves, that we can be saved.
